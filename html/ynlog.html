How It Works:

Simply put the tarot isn’t designed for straight “yes” or “no” questions. But sometimes they're the type of question you need answered, and fast. That’s how “Ask The Tarot Yes or No” came to be.

It’s your own personal reader – with you 24/7 – offering clarity, context & the wisdom of the tarot when you’re faced with a tricky decision to make.

To start, you’ll need to be logged in and have added some tokens to your account. Once that’s done, reflect on your question & then simply hit the button.

The deck is shuffled and cut once, revealing the card that holds the answer to your question.

You will get a “yes” or “no”, but you’ll also get an interpretation of the card’s meaning to help guide you in making your final decision.
