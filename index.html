How Does It Work?

Select your reading from the readings page - each reading card has all the information you'll need for that reading.

Hit the "Ask Now" button to proceed to the checkout.

For the General & Bundle readings, you'll have the option at the checkout to ask any specific questions you'd like answered for your chosen topic.

Once you've successfully checked out, your request for a reading is added to the queue.

The cards will be consulted & your reading will be completed in the next few days, usually within 48 hours of being received.

The reading is recorded & an audio file & image of the spread will be emailed to you.

General or Specific?

It's entirely a matter of personal choice. If there's a particular problem or issue you'd like to address then a specific reading is your best option as that will be the entire focus of the reading.

If you're after a broader view of a particular part of your life, then there's a general reading in the mix somewhere with your name on it.

Perhaps you feel something is awry or not as it should be, but you're not entirely sure of the details? A general Revelation reading is for you - a few rounds of simple questions thrown at the cards will help set the actual reading on the right course.
