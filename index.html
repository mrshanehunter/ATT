Focal Chakra
Base Chakra - Family

Belonging - Community - Culture - Tribe

All living creatures have at least one thing in common – we all come from “family”. Focus on the energy that envelops your family and its connection to you: who you are & where you are from.

Think beyond immediate “blood-relatives” because the family or tribe you create for yourself is just as important when it comes to this energy, and how it’s currently serving you.

We inherit a lot of beliefs & values from our families, often without question. Now is the time to question those that limit or restrict you from being your true self. Recognise & appreciate each individual (you included) as an integral part of the family unit that offers a sense of belonging & the freedom to be yourself.

How Is Family Affecting Your Base Chakra?

Feeling proud of your heritage and family, secure & stable, connection with community, a right to be on earth and a strong sense of belonging are all indicators that the family element isn’t helping keep your base chakra vibrating harmoniously.

Feeling limited by family values, insecure or fearful, out of place or separate, or unable to let go of the past can all be signs that your base chakra has an imbalance due to family.
